Welcome to a massage session to care for, maintain, and relax your body while supporting your overall well-being! I also treat symptoms caused by temporomandibular muscle disorders. If needed, I provide tips and guidance during the massage to help alleviate and prevent pain. Additionally, I offer kinesio and sports taping.

Welcome to physiotherapy if you find that movement and/or work has become difficult due to pain or if you need rehabilitation following an injury or illness. You can also visit if you need guidance on preventing musculoskeletal disorders. Physiotherapy includes an interview, examination, active exercises, and/or other treatment methods depending on your condition, preferences, and the goals we set together.

I am a physiotherapist (Jamk, Laurea) and a certified massage therapist (Sport Massage Institute of Finland). Additionally, I have completed SUHO's temporomandibular muscle massage training.
